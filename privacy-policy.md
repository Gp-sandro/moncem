# Moncem Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

## What We Collect

Moncem collects the minimum account and product data needed to run a founder community:

- Email address
- Full name
- Username
- Bio
- Location
- Avatar URL
- Building stage
- Interests
- Connect status
- Push notification token
- Post content, including title, excerpt, body, tags, milestone, and cover image URL
- Reactions you give to posts
- Conversations you participate in
- Messages you send
- Notifications you receive
- Reports you file
- Beta feedback you submit

## Why We Collect It

We use this data to authenticate you, show your profile, personalize builder discovery, publish your posts, deliver reactions and messages, send notifications, protect the community from abuse, and improve the beta product.

## Where It Is Stored

Moncem stores data in Supabase Auth, Supabase Postgres, Supabase Storage, and Supabase Edge Functions. Supabase infrastructure runs on AWS. The exact project region should be confirmed in the Supabase dashboard before publishing an App Store privacy listing.

## Who Can See It

Other authenticated Moncem users can see your public profile fields, posts, and public reaction counts. Conversation participants can see messages inside their shared conversation. You can see your own notifications, beta feedback, and reports. Moncem administrators may review reports and operational logs when needed to keep the service safe. Push tokens are used only by server-side notification functions.

## How Long We Keep It

We keep your data until you delete it or delete your account. Reports may be retained as an abuse-prevention audit trail where required to protect the community.

## How To Delete It

You can delete your account in the app from Settings -> Delete account. This permanently removes your profile, posts, reactions, conversations, messages, notifications, beta feedback, and authentication account.

## How To Export It

You can request a data export in the app from Settings -> Download my data. The export includes your profile, posts, reactions, conversations, sent messages, received notifications, reports filed, and beta feedback.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact privacy@moncem.app.
